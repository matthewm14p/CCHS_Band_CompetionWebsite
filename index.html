What Is Band?

"A band is more than the sum of its parts. You can list off all these things you can do in band, you work hard, you are disciplined, you have fun, you play music, but at the end of the day, you are family." - Mr. Montgomry


What Do You Do In Band?

There are many things to do in band, including:


♪ Learning to play an Instrument

♪ Learning to read Music

♪ Working with your peers

♪ Competing in Marching and Concert

♪ Becoming Responsible

♪ Performing for your families and the community

♪ Etc.


How to Join:

♪ Speak with Mr. Montgomery or Ms. Sain to get more information.
